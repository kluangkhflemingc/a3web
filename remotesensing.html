The Joys of Spectral Imagery

Remote Sensing allows us to detect objects from afar. Let our Remote Sensing Specialist help you observe areas that may be out of reach.

An Imagery Analysis Tool For Any Job

With spectral imagery there is nothing that can't be analyzed. Orbital satellites give us the ability to detect phenomena that are invisible to the human eye. Some of these things include soil moisture content, crop and forest health, the detection of acient ruins and many, many more.

Different Imagery Analysis

From the visible light spectrum we see everyday to the rays on either side, we can monitor global environmental health. The different frequencies require different imagery bands to be observed, but once collected can be used to show a unique display with detailed information.
